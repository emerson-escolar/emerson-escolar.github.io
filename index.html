Hello!

I am an assistant professor at the Division of human environmental science (mathematics and informatics), Graduate School of Human Development and Environment, Kobe University.

My research centers around developing theory and tools for topological data analysis. In particular, I work with representation theory, algebraic topology, algorithms and computation, and the many ways these different topics can be put together.
