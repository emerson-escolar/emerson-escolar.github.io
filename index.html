Hello!

I am currently an assistant professor at Kobe University Graduate School of Human Development and Environment.

I was previously a postdoctoral researcher of the RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project in the Hiraoka Laboratory based in the Kyoto University Institute for Advanced Study.

My research centers around developing theory and tools for topological data analysis. In particular, I work with representation theory, algebraic topology, algorithms and computation, and the many ways these different topics can be put together.
